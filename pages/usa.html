    APPLYING FOR YOUR STUDENT F1 VISA

    Every USA student applicant must attend an interview for an F1 Student Visa on a date and time scheduled for it at any of the designated US – Embassy in your country of residence. The pattern of the interview is Face-to-Face and cannot be done by proxy. But children below 17 years of age can attend interview with their parents, guardian or sponsor.

    USA Student visa interview

    USA visa interview is the last bus stop one-on-one assessment opportunity availed unto a student visa applicant to justify why he should not be denied an F1 student visa.

    F1-Student Visa Requirements

    • A valid travel passport,
    • A valid i-20 form (to indicate admission acceptance to study in an approved USA school),
    • Service Fee Receipt (i-90 form). (This indicates that the Student hand Exchange Visitors Information System has been complied),
    • A valid Confirmation of Appointment,
    • A valid DS Confirmation Certificate,
    • Original and Copies of Student’s Academic Credentials or Evaluated Transcripts,
    • References / recommendation letters,
    • Financial statement showing sufficient funds
    • Proof of visa fee receipt paid, and
    • Passport photograph.
