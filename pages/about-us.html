About Us

ABOUT RONALD AND PARTNERS

10+ Years of Your Trust and Recommendation

Ronald and Partners Consult is a one-stop-shop immigration consulting firm in Africa with expansive specialty in diverse shades of immigration matters including rendering support services to skilled & unskilled migrants across board, overseas university admission placements services and general visa support services. The firm also specializes in visa counseling, group tour and travels support services for corporate bodies and individuals.

Recently, Ronald and Partners Consult in collaboration with Royal Field Solicitors have made giant stride in supervising investors’ migration visa, golden visa and second passport for alternate residency purposes. We also handle Nigeria expatriate paper woks and residency ratifications such as processing of business permit/establishment grants, CERPAC card, and expatriate family residency visa including Niger wife visa.

Over the years, we have nursed a great industry to the extent that Ronald and Partners Consult has been judged by many as one of the fastest growing immigration consulting firms across Sub-Sahara Africa with a focus on service excellence and client’s satisfaction.

OUR MISSION

To apply high expertise and professional diligence in providing our services to all our clients and sustaining good business relationship with our partners

OUR GOAL

To become the leader and one of the most efficient and effective one-stop-shop immigration consulting firms in Africa. We intend to pursue this objective by supporting our clients and applicants through their applications for skilled and unskilled migration visa, placing students into lofty career driving courses and programmes in our partner Universities across the world, providing expert visa and travel support services to deserving applicants and helping foreign expatriate settle within their neighborhood in West Africa and also assisting in migrating expatriate families into the West Africa region; while ensuring that they properly comply with applicable immigration rules and residency duties.
