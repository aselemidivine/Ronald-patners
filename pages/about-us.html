ABOUT RONALD AND PARTNERS

10+ Years of Your Trust and Recommendation

Establish in 2010, and precisely registered under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in November 2010; Ronald and Partners Consult has sustained a good reputation in dispensing quality and professional immigration consulting and overseas education placement services to clients and prospective students in Nigeria and across the world.

We have a team of well trained staff that is well oiled with the right team leadership under Barr. Ronald Omozuyi, a seasoned international immigration attorney in Nigeria with wealth of experience in immigration laws and cross border protocols.

We will continue to remain grateful to our clients across the world for their continued loyalty, support and referrals.

We are also using this medium to appreciate our global partners including the Universities, Colleges and partner firms across the world for standing by us and for given us the opportunity to promote their programmes and objectives in Africa.

Going into the future, we look up to expanding our operations base beyond borders and also ensuring that our clients secures the best of satisfactions from our services. Now, we are better positioned to continue in our goodwill in rendering the best of professional services to all our clients across the world.

We will continue to be that firm in Africa that will give you the very honest and appropriate advice irrespective of not getting the brief at the end.

OUR MISSION

To apply high expertise and professional diligence in providing our services to all our clients and sustaining good business relationship with our partners accross the world.

OUR GOAL

To become one of the leading and most efficient one-stop-shop immigration consulting firms in Africa. We intend to pursue this goal through intensive researches, professional developments, and technological innovations. Our service areas will continue to expand around study migration programmes, skilled migration programmes, visas, and investments and immigration residencies.
