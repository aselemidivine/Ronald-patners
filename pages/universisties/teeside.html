    Study in the UK at University Of Teeside

    Teesside University is a leading public institution in England with two main campuses in Middlesbrough and London City. The institution has successfully transformed her campus in Middlesborugh to one of the most students friendly environment in the world which saw the her winning the award as the No. 1 institution for international student’s satisfaction in 2022.

    The University is strongly reputed in the areas of forensic science, Engineering, Health Science, Business and Management, Accounting, Computer Games Design and Animation, IT Project Management, Business Analytic, Data Analytic, Sport and exercise, and many more for Undergraduate and Postgraduate Studies.

    The University has a £5,000Pounds Vice Chancellor scholarship that is open for any student to compete and is currently considering restoring her usual £2,000 to £3,000 scholarships fund for international students.

    TEESSIDE’S NOTABLE ACHIEVEMENTS AND AWARDS

    TIMES HIGHER EDUCATION WORLD UNIVERSITY RANKING 2020: Teesside University is ranked in the 801-1000 band of global universities in the Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2020. These include almost 1,400 universities across 92 countries, standing as the largest and most diverse university rankings ever to date
    GOLD AWARD: Teesside University has achieved Gold status from EcoCampus – the leading environmental management system and award scheme for the higher and further education sectors. The EcoCampus gold award sits alongside the University’s Carbon Trust Standard, an award that is given to organisations who strive to measure, manage and reduce carbon emissions across their own operations.
    RNIB - VISIBLY BETTER EMPLOYER: Teesside is the first University outside of London to achieve Visibly Better Employer. To gain the accreditation the University demonstrated its commitment to becoming an inclusive employer for blind and partially sighted people. This means that you can be confident that the recruitment process will be fair and inclusive.
    EXCELLENCE IN RESEARCH AWARD: The University has retained the European Commission’s HR in Excellence in Research Award following a two year self-assessment. The award is given to organisation which can demonstrate a robust and public implementation strategy of improving the career development of researchers. The UK process incorporates both the QAA Quality Code for Higher Education and the Concordat to Support the Career Development of Researchers.

    For International Students

    Teesside University provides a meet and greet service for international students arriving from overseas and an orientation programme will also help new arrivals settle into the area. Talks and workshops with local and University authorities, as well as meetings with international student advisors, will help provide support throughout the year. Pre-sessional and in-sessional English language courses are available for students who wish to raise their English level, as well as the more informal International English Language Cafe run by current students.

    Accommodation

    Teesside University guarantees a place in University accommodation to all international students. There is also accommodation available for postgraduate and mature students and Teesside has some of the most affordable university accommodation in the UK, including on-campus halls of residence and off-campus managed houses. There are a little over 1,100 rooms available, with en suite and standard options, generally arranged in groups of 4-11 per shared kitchen.
